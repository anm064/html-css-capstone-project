Historical Facts About Chihuahuas

Chihuahuas are often viewed as ankle-biting, loud barking, annoying lapdogs. But anyone who has had a chihuahua knows this isn't completely true.so what are some facts about the chihuahuas history.


  1. The Chihuahua Club of America, founded in 1923, was created to develop a community of Chihuahua breeders and further the breed in the United States.
  2. There are two types of chihuahuas a smooth coat or a long coat
  3. Chihuashuas were bred for warm weather
  4. chihuahuas are the smallest dogs in the world
  5. the average chihuahua life expectancy is 14 to 16 years
